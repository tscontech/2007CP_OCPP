RTC

This is the driver for RTC.

The driver sources are put at /sdk/driver/itp/rtc*.c. Its parent driver is itp_rtc.c

  • Functions

    1. read()

      Reads RTC second value. Will call to itpRtcGetTime().

    2. write()

      Writes RTC second value. Will call to itpRtcSetTime().

    3. ioctl()
      1. ITP_IOCTL_INIT Inizializes this module. Will call to itpRtcInit().
  • Configurations

    1. CFG_RTC_ENABLE

      Determines build/link this module or not.

    2. CFG_RTC_MODULE

      Determines which sdk/driver/itp/rtc/itp_rtc_<module>.c will be built/linked.
